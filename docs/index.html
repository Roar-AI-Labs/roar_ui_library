Brand Direction

Calm workspace UI: cool neutral surfaces, a swappable accent for actions and information, and category steps 2–6 as grayscale. Gamified tiers stay soft so the default tone stays business-ready.

Calm accent

Colors

Foundation is neutral (surfaces, borders, body text). The accent ramp comes from the palette you choose under Appearance — default is grayscale Neutral; add Slate (blue), Teal, Violet, Amber, or Rose when you want hue, in light or dark mode.

These tokens update when you change Accent palette under Appearance. Actions, links, focus, and informational callouts share this ramp (no separate “info” hue).

Modals & overlays

Pop-ups use a full-screen scrim (.rs-modal-overlay) with position: fixed so the dimmed layer covers the viewport (in this demo the dialog markup lives at the end of <body> so it is not clipped by animated layout sections). The dialog (.rs-modal) is centered in the scrim. Click the backdrop to dismiss, press Esc, or use the close control. Layouts: default (narrow form), width utilities (.rs-modal--md / --lg / --xl), and the split settings shell (.rs-modal--settings with .rs-settings-* regions). In production, trap focus inside the dialog and restore focus to the opener on close.
